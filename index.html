About Me

I am a Montessori educator specialized in working with children aged 3 to 6, a crucial stage in human development where the foundations for lifelong learning, independence, and social-emotional skills are built. My passion lies in creating an environment that nurtures each child's natural curiosity and potential, guiding them through this formative period with respect and understanding.

My approach integrates the Montessori method with research-based practices such as read-aloud strategies and positive discipline, fostering a nurturing and respectful learning environment. With international experience in Italy, Spain, and Germany, I aim to provide children with autonomy, confidence, and a love for learning.
